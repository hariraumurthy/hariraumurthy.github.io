Research
Most recently, I fixed, a result of McCarthy and Minasian and generalized the Hochschild-Kostant-Rosenberg(HKR) theorem to factorization homology. My other projects over the past six years have involved the Adams/May spectral spectral sequence computations, tmf computations, algebraic K-theory, 2-vector bundles/2-characters, Hopkins-Kuhn-Ravenel-Stapleton character theory, the Bismut-Chern character, and factorization homology.
